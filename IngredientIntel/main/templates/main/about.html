About Ingredient Intel

Ingredient Intel is a platform dedicated to providing valuable information about various ingredients used in food and products. Our mission is to empower consumers, researchers, and companies to make informed decisions.

Our team consists of passionate individuals who are dedicated to researching and providing accurate and up-to-date information about ingredients and their effects on health, environment, and society.
